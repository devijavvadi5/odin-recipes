Description

Biryani is one of the most loved South Indian recipe. Its aroma and taste grabs the attention across the world !

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice - 2 cups (soaked for 30 minutes)
  • Chicken - 500g (preferably bone-in)
  • Onions - 2 large (thinly sliced)
  • Tomatoes - 2 medium (chopped)
  • Yogurt/Curd - 1/2 cup
  • Oil or Ghee - 3-4 tbsp
  • Water - as needed for boiling rice
  • Salt - to taste

Spices

  • Ginger-Garlic paste – 1.5 tbsp
  • Green chilies – 2–3 (slit)
  • Red chili powder – 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder – 1/2 tsp
  • Coriander powder – 1 tsp
  • Biryani masala – 1 tbsp (or Garam masala)
  • Bay leaves – 2
  • Cinnamon stick – 1 inch
  • Cardamom – 3–4
  • Cloves – 3–4
  • Star anise – 1
  • Black peppercorns – 6–8
  • Shah jeera (caraway seeds) – 1/2 tsp
  • Mace – a small piece (optional)

Step by Step Process

  1. Soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices & salt (30 mins).
  3. Fry sliced onions until golden brown (Birista).
  4. In a pan, cook marinated chicken with tomatoes and green chilies until tender.
  5. Boil rice with whole spices until 70% cooked; drain.
  6. Layer chicken and rice in a pot. Add fried onions, mint, coriander, saffron milk, and lemon juice.
  7. Cover tightly and cook on dum (low flame) for 15–20 minutes.
  8. Let it rest for 10 minutes, then gently mix and serve hot.
